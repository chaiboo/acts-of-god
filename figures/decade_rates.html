Acts of God · New York Court of Appeals · 1847–2017

The year the doctrine stopped doing work was not the year it stopped being cited.

Across 170 years of New York's highest court, the act of God defense appears in 90 reported opinions. But the grammar of those invocations shifts decisively after 1916: live adjudication effectively ends, while boilerplate recitation of the common-carrier formula persists for another sixty years as a fossil.
